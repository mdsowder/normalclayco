Meet Maggie

Normal Clay Co. is run by Maggie Sowder — full-time mom, part-time ceramist, and enthusiastic market browser. Most pieces are thrown in a home studio after bedtime, one batch at a time.

Maggie loves trading handmade goods with friends, sourcing clay and tools locally, and making work that feels a little wobbly and very loved. If you’d like the longer version of the story, you can read more on the About page.
