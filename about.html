Normal Clay Co. is Maggie.

Hi, I’m Maggie Sowder. I’m a full-time mom, part-time ceramist, and long-time believer that the things we use every day should feel special and a little bit strange.

Most of this work is made in a small home studio, in the quiet hours after bedtime or between tutoring sessions and grocery runs. I started making pottery as a way to slow down, trade pieces with friends, and bring more handmade things into our kitchen.

I love local markets, messy studio shelves, and clay that remembers the touch of a hand. Normal Clay Co. leans into wobble and texture — every mug, plate, or bowl is a little different, the way people are.

  • Small, seasonal batches instead of constant restocks.
  • Clay and tools sourced as locally as possible.
  • Pieces meant to be used, chipped, loved, and passed around the table.
Made slowly in Ypsilanti, Michigan
